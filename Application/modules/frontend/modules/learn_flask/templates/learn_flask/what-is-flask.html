What is Flask?

  • Flask is a web application framework, wrapping the Jinja 2 templating engine and the Werkzueg toolkit for WSGI interfaces.
  • Flask came out of an April Fool's joke but proved popular enough to make into a serious application in its own right
  • Although Flask has grown in popularity over the past 2 years, it is still very immature, and has not had a major release yet.

Flask makes heavy use of Python language features, such as decorators. This makes implementing a Flask WebApp really quick and simple to do.
